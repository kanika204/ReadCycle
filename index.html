"Books, once read, become a part of us.
Sharing them is sharing a part of ourselves."

READ CYCLE offers significant benefits to users by providing a cost-effective solution for acquiring books. It enables users to buy, sell, or exchange books at reduced prices, making reading and learning more affordable. This platform promotes sustainability by extending the lifecycle of books, reducing waste, and minimizing the environmental impact of producing new books. Users can declutter their homes by selling unused books while helping others access valuable resources.
Additionally, READ CYCLE fosters a sense of community among readers and learners, creating a shared space for exchanging knowledge. With its user-friendly interface and convenience, it streamlines the process of finding or listing books, making it an efficient and valuable platform for all book lovers.

Why to Exchange Books?

Simple

Read Cycle is a simple to use platform that is easy for booklovers of all age groups to discover the joy of reading. Just give one and you can take one we have, and then enjoy reading. Be the smart aleck- exchange in easy steps.

Save

Read Cycle is a social commerce enterprise where booklovers share their treasures. At bookexchange you only have to pay for shipping of its original cost. Be the miser- save money when you exchange.

Socialise

Rediscover the joy of sharing, and socialising with books. Any book in your collection, could be on someone else must read list. A unique opportunity to give your books a new lease of life... let them fly. Be the socialist- share your treasures when you exchange.

Convenient

No more stepping out to buy books... we pick up and deliver the books at your doorstep. We will do the leg work for you. Be the eco warrior - save trees when you exchange.

Less Carbon Footprint

Let's leave a lasting legacy and not more carbon footprint! The more we recycle and exchange we not only reduce the stress on our forests, but also earn good karma by sharing what we have. Be the legend- leave a clean footprint when you exchange.

Less for more

Classics, best sellers and even some rare books that cost a fortune are here at bookxchange for you. Just exchange your old well-read books by just paying for shipping. Fortune at a fraction...go for it. Be the bounty hunter- gather treasures when you exchange.

Less Clutter

Less clutter means more space. Where space is a premium, by proactively exchanging your books you not only save space but also avoid clutter. Be the minimalist- clean your house when you exchange.

Less Paper...more trees

The best way to save the environment is to plant a tree - the next best way is to save a tree from being cut. Research shows that we save one tree for every 65 books recycled. Be the eco warrior - save trees when you exchange.
